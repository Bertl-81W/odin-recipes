Nonna's Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is our Nonna's very special Lasagna recipe

The recipe has been in our family for generations and has never been altered

Ingredients

  • Dry Lasagna Noodle
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • 2 Eggs
  • Italian Herbs
  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Italian Sausage

Steps

    Boil Noodles till 'al dente', strain and rinse with cold water. Pat dry

    Mix together Ricotta, eggs and Italian Herbs.

    cook ground Beef and Sausage till done. Internal temperature of 165F

    place a shallow layer of sauce and spread on baking dish, this prevents Lasagna sticking to dish

    assemble Lasagna, first place a layer of noddles into bottom of baking dish till surface is covered (it's ok to break a noodle to fit)

    add a layer of Ricotta and egg mixture

    spread your meats over the layer of Ricotta mix

    top with sauce and shredded cheeses, and repeat these steps till you reach top rim of baking dish

    finish with topping your lassagna with another ladle of sauce and top with shredded cheeses

    cover with parchment paper and aluminum foil, and bake on 375F for 35min

    uncover lasagna and let it finish in the oven for approx. 5min or till cheese on top is golden brown

    let rest for 10min and serve
