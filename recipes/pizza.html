Standard Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a yummy Pizza recipe

The recipe has been doctored up over years to our liking

Ingredients

  • 00 Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • San Marzano Tomato
  • Oregano
  • Fresh Basil
  • Pinch of Salt

Steps

    preheat oven to 450F

    make dough and let sit overnight(please follow our Pizza dough recipe

    flourwork surface, roll and stretch out your dough

    crush San Marzano Tomatoes into a bowl with your hands, stir in a teaspoon of dry Oregano

    spread crushed Tomatoes across dough in circular fashion

    top with shredded Cheeses and evenly spread out dollops of Ricotta Cheese, no bigger then a tablespoon

    place on middle rack of oven and bake for approx. 15-17min.

    let Pizza rest for 5min, slice and serve with dry hot chile flakes
