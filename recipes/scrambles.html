Simple Scrambled Egg Recipe

Description

This is our simple Scrambled Eggs recipe

The recipe is easy to follow and is very yummy.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole Eggs
  • 2 Egg Whites
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

    Mix together milk, whole eggs and egg whites.

    Preheat your skillet and heat a tsp Olive Oil with tablespoon of butter.

    add egg mixture and cook till done and solid on one side, then flip eggs

    egg another minute abnd tear apart them apart with an non-stick cooking utensil like a spatula

    sprinkle with Black Pepper and serve
